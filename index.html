Art of web development

A web developer always has a clear vision!

They enjoy researching about new technologies, learning about best practices, implementing captivating designs, and discovering new interesting ways to make exactly what they have envisioned work exactly the way they want it to.

There are very few words able to describe the exciting feeling a developer gets when implementing a new feature proves to be a success.

The business of web development has reached the point of where simply 'making it work' doesn't give the developers, and clients, the same sattisfaction as when they actually face a challange in their designs.

A new feature, a new problem, a new challange. A new opportunity to figure out the best way to minimize the damage to the already existing code.

Fix it, break it, fix it, break it again, learn about something new, implement it... Fix it, break it, fix it, break it again.

About me

I am one of those web developers that have a clear vision, an ever-growing appetite for learning, and a child-like excitement when everything falls into place and just works !

I write clean code, and I take time to carefully read documentation. I am also an especially fast researcher. If I see something I like, and I have never done it before, and I can't seem to make it work on my own... I will do everything in my power to solve it! I will even click the google page number two!

I am not only a web developer though. I am a person with ambition, and aspirations. I am also a poet, a reader, an english teacher, an artist, gamer, a cat person... and a dreamer!
